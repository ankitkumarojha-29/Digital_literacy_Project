Stay Safe Online: Prevention Checklist

1. Never share your UPI PIN with anyone; remember that a PIN is only required to send money, never to receive money.
2. Set daily transaction limits on your UPI applications to minimize potential financial losses in case of a security breach.
3. Ignore 'urgent' calls or SMS messages claiming your bank account or SIM card will be blocked; always verify by calling the official customer care number yourself.
4. Do not download screen-sharing applications (like AnyDesk or TeamViewer) if instructed to do so by an unknown caller.
5. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) for all your email, social media, and university portal accounts.
6. Avoid using public, unsecured Wi-Fi networks (like at cafes or railway stations) to make financial transactions.
7. Regularly update your laptop and mobile operating systems to ensure you have the latest security patches.
8. If you suspect any fraudulent activity, immediately report it to the National Cyber Crime Portal at cybercrime.gov.in or call the Helpline 1930.
